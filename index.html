What We're Building

A Facility Built for the Game

First Coast Roller Hockey is a community-driven project to establish a permanent, professional-grade roller hockey destination in the Jacksonville area. No more driving hours for ice time or making do with parking lots.

🏒

Regulation Rink

Full-size sport court surface designed specifically for inline and roller hockey play.

🏆

Year-Round Leagues

Adult and youth leagues across skill levels — beginner to competitive — with structured seasons and playoffs.

🎓

Training & Clinics

Skill development programs, skating clinics, goalie training, and youth development camps.

🛼

Open Skate

Public skate sessions for the whole family — no hockey experience required to enjoy the rink.

🏬

Pro Shop

On-site equipment sales, rentals, skate sharpening, and gear customization.

🤝

Community Space

Locker rooms, spectator seating, a lounge area, and room for tournaments and events.

Why Jacksonville

The First Coast Deserves This

The Jacksonville metro is one of the fastest-growing regions in the Southeast — and one of the only major markets without a dedicated roller hockey facility. Our players travel to Orlando or Tampa just to find competitive games.

We're changing that. With your support and demonstrated community interest, we can move forward with securing a location, locking in financing, and breaking ground. Every registration matters.
