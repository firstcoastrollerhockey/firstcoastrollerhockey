Milestone

Website Launch

The First Coast Roller Hockey website is officially up.

This is the first real step toward building a dedicated home for roller hockey in our area — something players, families, and the community have gone without for far too long.

Right now, the site is focused on two things:

  • Sharing the vision
  • Proving the demand

There's a simple sign-up form on the site. That list matters more than anything else at this stage. Every name shows that this community is real, active, and ready for a rink of its own.

If you want to see this happen, take a minute to add your name. It's a small step that helps move a big idea forward.

Check it out, share it, and help us build this the right way — from the ground up.
