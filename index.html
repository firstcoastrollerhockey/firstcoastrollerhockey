What We're Building Together

A Place That Belongs to All of Us

First Coast Roller Hockey is being built from the ground up by people who love this game and love this city. The features and programs listed here represent our vision — what we're working toward and what we believe this community deserves. What we're able to deliver will depend on interest, funding, and costs, but every decision we make will put the community first.

🏒

Your Home Rink

A full-size, regulation sport court surface built specifically for roller hockey — finally right here in Jacksonville where you live.

🏆

Leagues for Everyone

Adult and youth leagues at every skill level, from your very first game to your most competitive season. There's a spot on the schedule for you.

🎓

Grow the Game

Skating clinics, youth camps, and skill development programs — because a great hockey community is built one player at a time, starting with the youngest ones.

🛼

Open to All

Never laced up skates? That's exactly the point. Open skate sessions are for the whole family — no experience, no team, no problem. Just show up.

🏬

Everything You Need

Everything you need on-site — quality gear and a pro shop run by people who actually play.

🤝

A Real Gathering Place

Locker rooms, seating for families, a lounge to hang after the game, and space for tournaments, birthday parties, and community events. This is your second home.

Why This Matters

Jacksonville Has Always Deserved This

Right now, First Coast roller hockey players drive two hours to Orlando or Tampa just to find a competitive game in a real roller hockey facility. That shouldn't be the story of roller hockey in a metro this size.

We're changing that — but only with you. Every person who signs up sends a clear message: Jacksonville is ready. Your name on this list is the foundation this rink is built on.
