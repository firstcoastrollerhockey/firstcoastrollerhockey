About the Project

First Coast Roller Hockey is a planned roller hockey facility for the First Coast area. Our goal is to create a dedicated space for leagues, training, and community events — something that currently doesn't exist locally. We're starting by gauging community interest so we can show the real demand that exists here in Jacksonville.
We are exploring potential locations in the Jacksonville and St. Johns County area. The exact location will depend on finding a space that fits the project's needs — size, accessibility, and long-term growth potential. Interestingly, where you live matters: the ZIP codes showing the most interest will directly influence where we look first.
We are still in the early phases of the project. The timeline depends on community interest, funding, and securing the right location. We'll share updates as meaningful progress is made — everyone who signs up will hear from us first.

The Facility

That is the goal, but it's not guaranteed yet. We would love to build a full indoor, climate-controlled facility — especially important here in Florida. However, what ultimately gets built will depend on community support, funding, and overall project costs. Our priority is simple: build a dedicated roller hockey rink for the community, even if that means starting with a high-quality outdoor or covered rink first.
The final version depends on the level of support and resources we're able to secure. Our ideal vision is a full indoor facility with climate control, leagues, dedicated training space, a pro shop, locker rooms, and comfortable areas for players and families. That said, we're flexible in how we get there. If needed, we're open to building a well-designed outdoor or partially covered rink as a first step — something real and usable that we can grow from.
We are planning for a high-quality roller hockey surface designed for both performance and player safety. The final surface choice will depend on the type of facility we build, but player experience is the top priority in that decision.
